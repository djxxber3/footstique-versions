Privacy Policy for FS Player

Last updated: September 10, 2025

Footstique operates the FS Player mobile application.

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you directly. However, our Service uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

The primary third-party service used by our app is:

  • Google AdMob: This is an advertising service provided by Google Inc. AdMob uses your device's Advertising ID to serve personalized advertisements. It may also collect other device-related information such as IP address, device type, and operating system to provide its services.

We recommend you review the privacy policy of Google for more detailed information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected by our third-party partners is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service: The ads served help support the operational costs of the app, allowing us to offer it for free.
  • To serve personalized advertising: To show you ads that are more relevant to your interests.
  • For analytics: To understand how users interact with the ads and to improve the advertising experience.

3. Your Choices and Opt-Out Rights

We respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to control personalized advertising. You can opt out of ad personalization through your device settings. This action will not stop you from seeing ads, but they will be less relevant to your interests.

  • On Android: Open your device's Settings app > Google > Ads > and enable Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • On iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > Advertising > and enable Limit Ad Tracking.

4. App Permissions

Our application requests the following permissions to function correctly:

  • android.permission.INTERNET: Allows the app to connect to the internet to stream video content and fetch advertisements.
  • android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Allows the app to check the status of the network connection.

5. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to take the necessary actions.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
